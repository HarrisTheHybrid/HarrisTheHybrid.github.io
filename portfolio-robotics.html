Lancer Robotics Description

The Lancer Robotics team is a 15 member Livingston Highschool team competing in the FIRST Technology Challenge (FTC). During my years on the team, the team has won the World Champion Think Award three times in a row from 2015 - 2017. The Think Award is awarded to the team with the best documentation of their robot. As the team's captain, I worked on building the robot, organizing outreach event to engage with the Livingston community, and supervised the junior members of the team. During my time in FTC, I was a competitive Dean's List Semi-Finalist.
